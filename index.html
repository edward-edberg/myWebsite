Bio

Edward Edberg is a Data Scientist at Asuransi Astra and a Remote Researcher at Boston University where he explores the integration of language and vision from multimodal sources such as text and images to construct knowledge and utilize it to create a system that capable of understanding human languages.

Previously, He received a B.E in Physics Engineering from Surya University in 2018. Also, He has done international research collaboration with Haryadi S. Gunawi from the University of Chicago. Their joint effort produces a paper, called "LinnOS: Predictability on Unpredictable Flash Storage with a Light Neural Network", at OSDI 2020. Currently, He is doing research collaboration with Derry Wijaya from Boston University.
